About Marco

I am Marco – an artist shaped by two worlds: the restless rhythm of the city and the grounding silence of nature. My creations bridge these realms, merging tribal heritage with modern existence and exploring themes of identity, disconnection, and liberation. Each artwork is a dialogue between past and present, urging viewers to pause and reflect on their own place in a rapidly changing world.

Every piece is produced as a museum‑quality giclée print, hand‑signed and individually numbered, limited to only 50 editions worldwide.
